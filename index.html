Protocol Documentation

Codex

DRYHOLE is an entropy-driven extraction protocol. Users deploy energy into uncertain geological depths to discover hidden value — $VEIN. Extraction is probabilistic. Outcome is entropy-bound.

The protocol operates across five strata — Surface, Shale, Sandstone, Salt Dome, and Abyss. Each stratum carries its own energy cost, success probability, and yield range. Deeper is not always better. Uncertainty compounds with depth.

Every extraction outcome is recorded on-chain. Immutable. Unmanipulated. The ledger does not lie. Only a fraction return with VEIN.

Core Mechanics

Start with 100 ENERGY. Each extraction costs ENERGY based on your chosen stratum. Win streaks of 3+ award bonus VEIN. Five consecutive null yields trigger a hidden entropy adjustment. The protocol is not charitable — but it is fair.

$VEIN Token

$VEIN is the native yield token of the DRYHOLE protocol. It is extracted, not minted. Supply is governed by entropy. Follow @Dryholexyz for protocol updates.
